# Internet
a virtual network with its own [[Addressing (internet)]] and name scheme
a collection of:
- networks
- routers interconnecting networks
- hosts connected to networks



---
## Architecture

can be implemented with:
- ethernet
- token ring
- serial line
- apple talk
- etc.



---
## History

Department of Defense Advance Research Project Agency (DARPA) made a nationwide network to allow computer access to the different research centers
--avoid giving computer to each center/uni 
--commercial success in 90s

internet is doubling in size every 9 to 12 months

internet is increasing productivity
