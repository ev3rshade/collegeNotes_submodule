Definition: Complete logically correct argument that shows that something is true mathematically
- Understandable
- Written with proper notation


## Styles of Proofs
- Direct Proof - prove directly
- Proof by contradiction - prove that assumption doesn't work mathematically 
- Proof by contraposition - prove the opposite of what we're trying to prove proves what we are given
- Proof by induction - starting with a base case and proving using steps, then infinite steps that the statement is true
